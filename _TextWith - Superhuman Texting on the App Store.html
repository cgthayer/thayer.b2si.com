Description

Business texting!


We rebuilt texting from the ground up to help you get stuff done. Use your TextWith phone number to keep your work and personal messages separate. Great for sales, customer support, operations, recruiting, marketing and planning events.


Send and receive text messages with customers, contractors, and partners. Best of all, they won't need to install anything!


TextWith comes with advanced features that make you feel superhuman. Follow-up Reminders, Scheduled Messages, and Click Statuses. To name but a few.


Features:

* SEND LATER - Schedule your messages to send when you know your recipients will read them.

* TEMPLATES - Write the best text every time without manual copying and pasting. With TextWith templates, the right message for every occasion is one click away. 

* TEXT MARKETING CAMPAIGNS - Automate the delivery of personalized messages.

* TEXTING ASSISTANT - Keeps you on track reminding you to follow-up so you never forget to reply.

* TEAM ENABLED - Team collaboration means you’ll never miss a beat!

* MANAGE NOTIFICATIONS - Mark yourself available when you want to be notified a new message comes in with Push Notifications and unavailable when you are away so you are not disturbed when you’re not working.

* LISTS + PIPELINES - Organize contacts into lists to track them through your sales pipeline, on-boarding or customer support process.

* ARCHIVE MESSAGES - Archive completed chats to keep your inbox clear.

* CLICK TRACKING - Know when someone clicks on the links you sent them.

CRM - Keep information about your customers, prospects, and partners up to date and always send messages with the most up to date context.


Here is how businesses are using TextWith:


SALES

* Set reminders to follow up with each lead

* Organize contacts into lists to track them through your sales pipeline.

* A shared inbox where your team can share messages and replies


CUSTOMER SUPPORT

* Triage chats by assigning them to yourself other people. Get notifications for the chats most relevant to you

* Templates for commonly used messages, including text, photos, links, and even the first name of the contact

* Set scheduled message and never forget to follow up with a customer

* See when team members reply to a chat

* Add and share private notes about each contact


MARKETING

* Create campaigns to send private, personalized text messages.

* Personalize messages with merge tokens like "first name"

* Schedule messages to be sent at a specific date and time
